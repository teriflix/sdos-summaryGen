EXT. HILL OUTSIDE ASHTON - DAY

Edward confronts the giant Karl, who emerges from a cave, threatening to devour him, but instead reveals his crushing loneliness and hunger. As they sit together, Edward convinces Karl that he too is trapped in a small town, limiting his potential. The two form an unlikely bond, with Edward convincing Karl to leave with him, aiming for the big city where Karl can be seen as more than just a monstrous outcast; their handshake seals a new beginning, one that promises freedom and possibility, but also uncertainty.
