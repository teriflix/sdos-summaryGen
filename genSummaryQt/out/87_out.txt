As Edward stands before Sandra, the door creaks open, allowing him to step into her world, his bouquet of daffodils wilting at his feet, their bright yellow petals now dulled by the revelation that she's already promised another man. His nervous laughter echoes through the air as Sandra's amusement turns to contrition, and with a gentle touch on the doorframe, she offers an olive branch, but it's too late; Edward's face crumples, his bravado crumbling beneath the weight of her words, leaving him exposed and vulnerable in the face of her engagement.
