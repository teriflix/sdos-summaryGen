As Ping and her identical twin Jing sing their final, haunting duet, Edward, caught off guard by the twins' shocking appearance, finds himself momentarily stunned before regaining his footing to dispatch the guards. But as the audience erupts into applause, Edward slips away, only to be revealed as the parachuting enigma he was all along, leaving Ping and Jing bewildered in their spotlight, still entwined as one being, but now aware that their special bond comes with a price they're only just beginning to realize.
