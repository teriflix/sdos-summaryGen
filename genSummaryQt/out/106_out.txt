Sandra hangs sheets to dry on the clotheslines, forming a tunnel of fabric as she approaches the threshold of her life. A man's silhouette appears on one sheet, shattering her fragile calm, but when she turns to flee, it solidifies into Edward, his hand grasping for hers in a tender, desperate gesture, and she yields to the impossible, surrendering to their love just as we awaken to Josephine's gentle stir in bed, her gaze softening as Will watches over her with quiet devotion.
