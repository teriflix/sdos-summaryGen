Edward Bloom steps into the midst of Main Street's revelry, lanterns and streamers surrounding him as he's swept up in a chaotic dance number, townspeople grasping for his attention; however, when Jenny intervenes, he cuts through the music and abruptly exits, Beamen and Jenny in pursuit, their words laced with concern and longing, as Edward calmly asserts his departure, leaving behind a crowd of disbelieving onlookers, who can only watch as he walks away.
