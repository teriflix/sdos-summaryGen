Edward struggles through the dense thorns, his bare feet agonizing him, as ancient trees suddenly spring to life, their branches grasping for him like skeletal fingers; he leaps, dodges, and swings to evade the crushing darkness, his chain-breaking Key to the City lost in the mud; but with a burst of defiance, Edward stands, unbroken, as the forest's fury subsides, leaving him laughing, shoeless, and battered, yet somehow unscathed, as if he has transcended the night's terror into a defiant, mocking triumph.
