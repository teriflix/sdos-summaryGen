Will stands at the creaky porch, eyes fixed on the house with its identical white fence to his mother's, as the woman Jenny Hill's amateur piano playing drifts into view from within, growing discordant and frantic by the second. The cicadas' hum swells, punctuated by the sound of a child's footsteps receding into the distance; the piano student has fled, leaving Will and Jenny locked in an awkward encounter, each struggling to bridge the gap between their known past and this unexpected present.
